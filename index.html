ABOUT US

Nestled in the heart of North London, Jays Burger is a cherished bar and grill with a rich history and a dedication to comfort and quality. Established in the early 1960s by brothers Jaymie and Jayden Greenwood, our family-owned restaurant has been serving up delicious comfort food for generations.


At Jays Burger, our specialty is burgers. Juicy, flavorful, and made with love. We offer a wide range of burger variations, each crafted to perfection, along with succulent steaks that have become a local favorite. Our commitment to quality ingredients and exceptional taste shines through in every dish we serve.


What truly sets Jays Burger apart is our unwavering focus on making every customer feel right at home. From our warm, inviting atmosphere to our friendly staff, we prioritize your comfort and strive to create a dining experience that is both relaxing and memorable. Whether you're a long-time patron or a first-time visitor, you'll find a welcoming community and delicious comfort food waiting for you at Jays Burger.
